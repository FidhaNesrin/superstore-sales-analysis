## 📌 Project Overview
End-to-end retail sales analysis of 2,000+ orders using Python 
for EDA and Excel for interactive dashboard reporting.
Covers sales trends, regional performance, product profitability,
shipping analysis, and RFM customer segmentation.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions Answered
- Which region generates the most sales and profit?
- Which product categories and sub-categories are most profitable?
- How does discount rate impact profitability?
- Which shipping mode is most efficient?
- Who are our most valuable customers? (RFM Analysis)
- What is the monthly revenue trend over 4 years?

## 📊 Key Findings
- **West region** generated the highest revenue
- **Technology** category had the highest profit margin
- Discounts above **30%** consistently resulted in losses
- **Standard Class** shipping carried the most volume
- RFM analysis identified **Champions** as top 20% of customers
